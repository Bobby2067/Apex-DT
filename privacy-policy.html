Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

1. Introduction

APEX Driving School ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our P1 Eligibility System application ("the App").

By using our App, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

Identity Information

  • Full name (first name and surname)
  • Date of birth
  • Driver licence number
  • Driver licence expiry date

Contact Information

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Residential address

Driving Progress Information

  • Supervised driving hours (day and night)
  • Professional lesson hours
  • Course completions (Safer Driver, VRU, First Aid)
  • Assessment completion status (HPT, CBT&A, 1-22)
  • Final drive test results

Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Login timestamps and session data

Document Images (Optional)

  • Photos of CBT&A assessment sheets (for OCR processing)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To calculate your P1 provisional licence eligibility
  • To track and record your driving lesson progress
  • To manage bookings for final drive tests with ACT Government
  • To communicate with you about your driving progress
  • To comply with ACT road safety regulations and reporting requirements
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when you agree to this Privacy Policy
  • Contractual necessity: Processing is necessary to provide our driving instruction services
  • Legal obligation: We must comply with ACT road safety and licensing regulations
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our services and maintain security

5. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your personal information to:

  • ACT Government (Access Canberra): For final drive test bookings and licence applications
  • Service providers: Including our secure cloud hosting provider (Supabase) and authentication provider (Clerk)
  • Legal authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for 5 years after your final drive test, or 5 years from your last interaction with us if you do not complete a final drive test. This period is necessary to:

  • Comply with ACT road safety record-keeping requirements
  • Handle any disputes or legal claims that may arise
  • Provide historical records if you return for additional training

After the retention period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymised.

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in serious harm, we will:

  • Notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as soon as practicable
  • Notify affected individuals as required under the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme
  • Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach
  • Document the breach and our response in our breach register

10. Cookies and Tracking

Our App uses essential cookies and local storage to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Enable offline functionality

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

11. Information About Minors

Our App may process information about individuals under 18 years of age (learner drivers can be 16+). For users under 18, we require consent from a parent or guardian before collecting personal information. Parents/guardians may exercise any of the data rights on behalf of the minor.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Sending an email to your registered email address
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
